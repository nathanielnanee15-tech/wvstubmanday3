FAQs

How do I login?
  • Visit the school website and click the Login button.
  • Enter your Username/ID number and your password.
  • Click Login to access your dashboard.
How can I view my grades?
  • Log into your account.
  • Click “Grades” from the menu.
  • Your academic results will appear.
How do I check my attendance?
  • Login to your student profile.
  • Click on "Attendance".
  • You will see days present, absent, and tardy.
