Welcome Message from the Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to the official website of Wiliam V.S Tubman Gray High School. Our institution stands on a strong foundation of discipline, academic excellence, and moral values. We remain committed to providing quality education that empowers our students to become responsible citizens and future leaders of Liberia.

As we continue to grow, we encourage all students to stay focused, work hard, and uphold the principles that make our school unique. Parents and guardians, we appreciate your support and partnership in shaping the future of our children.

We invite you to explore our website and stay updated with school news, events, and important announcements.

Thank you for being part of our Eagle Kingdom.
