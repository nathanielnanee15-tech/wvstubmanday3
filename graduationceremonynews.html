Graduation Ceremony 2024

June 15, 2024 / School Grounds

The 2024 Graduation Ceremony celebrated the achievements of our students completing their academic journey at William V.S Tubman Gray High School.

Students received diplomas, and special awards were given for academic excellence, leadership, and community service. Parents and faculty joined to honor their success.

The event was highlighted with speeches, performances, and a sense of pride and accomplishment shared by the entire school community.
