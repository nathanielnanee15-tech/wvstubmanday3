About the School

Learn about our history, founding, values, and mission

Founding

William V.S Tubman Gray High School was founded in 1949 to provide quality secondary education in Liberia. Since then, it has grown to become one of the leading schools in the country, focusing on academic excellence and holistic student development.

History

The school has a long tradition of excellence in education. Over the decades, it has produced thousands of graduates who have contributed to society in various fields. Our historical milestones include development of the campus, introduction of innovative programs, and consistent achievement in national exams.

School Code & Values

  • Integrity and honesty in all actions
  • Respect for teachers, students, and the community
  • Commitment to academic excellence
  • Encouragement of creativity and critical thinking
  • Promotion of teamwork and social responsibility
